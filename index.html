What I Do

I deploy and administer servers for a large law firm with locations in multiple cities. Data-heavy work, virtualization, and database stuff are super interesting to me right now, and I like writing and working with quick, interesting web apps that take advantage of unusual data. I have varying degrees of experience with Javascript, Ruby, Python, various flavours of SQL, interacting with one of any number of APIs, and a little bit of everything else.
